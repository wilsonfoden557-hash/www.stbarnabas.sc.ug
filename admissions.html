Admissions

Join the St. Barnabas College family

Admission Information

  • Application Period: January - December 2027
  • Academic Year Starts: August 2026
  • Admission Fee: 50,000 UGX (Non-refundable)

Requirements

Academic Requirements

• Completed Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) with Good Aggregates
• Report cards from previous school (P.5 - P.7)
• Recommendation letter from previous school

Documents Needed

• Birth Certificate (Copy)
• Passport photos (4 copies)
• Parent/Guardian National ID (Copy)
• Medical form completed by a certified doctor

Age Requirements

• Senior 1: 12-14 years old
• Senior 2: 14-16 years old
• Senior 3: 15-17 years old
• Senior 4: 16-18 years old

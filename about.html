About St. Barnabas College Migadde

Education For Self Reliance

Our History

Founded in 1985, St. Barnabas College Migadde has been a beacon of academic excellence and moral formation in Uganda for over 38 years. What started as a small community school has grown into one of the region's most respected institutions.

Our Community

With over 1,200 students and 55 dedicated staff members, we create a nurturing environment where every student is known, valued, and supported. Our student-to-teacher ratio of 15:1 ensures personalized attention.

Our Achievements

We take pride in our students' outstanding performance, with a 76% pass rate in national examinations, numerous awards in sports and co-curricular activities, and recognition as one of the top performing schools.

Our Mission

To produce God fearing citizens who are morally upright and self reliant.

Our Vision

To be a model school where others come and learn.
